# campus-kart-
AI-powered campus marketplace — buy, sell, and reclaim lost items across your college community.
<br>
AI Sales Notes — a seller uploads a photo, Claude's vision reads the item's condition and brand, then auto-generates a title, description, and suggested price. The seller just edits and posts.
Lost & Found — users describe a lost item in plain English, the text is vectorized (semantic embedding), and Claude scans the found-items database for matches above an 80% threshold, then pushes a notification to both parties.
